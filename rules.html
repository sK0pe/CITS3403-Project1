Rules and Scoring

The objective of the game is to fill rows with blocks to score points.

Control Scheme

  • Press "ENTER" to start the game.
  • Press "SPACE" to pause and un-pause the game.
  • Blocks will enter the field of play one at a time.
  • Blocks descend at a set interval until they encounter the bottom of the field or they encounter another block.
  • Blocks cannot move up but the player can hasten their descent by pressing "S".
  • The player can move a block left (press "A") or right (press "D")
  • Blocks can be rotated (press "W") in a clockwise fashion to reposition.

Scoring

  • Blocks that finish their descent score 10 points.
  • If an entire row is filled with blocks (cash) the line disappears from public record to the offshore account, scoring 100 points bonus points.
  • If 2 or 3 lines are completed simultaneously, score 200 points and 300 bonus points respectively.
  • If the maximum possible lines (4) are filled simultaneously, score 800 bonus points.
  • Scoring 2 consecutive max (4) line clears results in a bonus of 1200 points!
